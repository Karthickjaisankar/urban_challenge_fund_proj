# Scheme KPI Research — MoHUA Cross-Department Dashboard

**Methodology & caveats.** For each of 54 schemes, the single most authoritative outcome/output indicator was selected — preferring metrics that the scheme's own portal, MIS, or annual report uses as its headline figure. Where a public dashboard exists (e.g. PM-JAY, Poshan Tracker, PM-KISAN, DILRMP, SVAMITVA), the KPI mirrors the portal's primary tile. Where no public dashboard exists (many state schemes), the KPI is derived from the scheme's stated objective and audit/PRS/RBI Study of State Finances reporting. Note: live portal verification was unavailable in this run; KPI selections reflect known scheme MIS conventions as of Jan 2026 and should be re-validated against each portal before publication. Cadence reflects the portal's update frequency.
